FACEIT Penalty Rules & FAQ Guide

How does the ban escalation system work?

FACEIT tracks "Leaver", "noShow", and "AFK/Declined" infractions under a single, unified account index. Cooldowns are automated and stack. Every infraction remains in your ban history for a rolling 30-day period. Multiple infractions within 30 days escalate your ban length significantly (up to 7 days).

What is the difference between Leaver, noShow, and AFK?

  • Leaver: Triggered if you are disconnected from a live server for a cumulative total of more than 5 minutes. Even if you reconnect and finish the match, the queue ban is applied at the end.
  • noShow: Triggered if you fail to connect to your assigned match server during the designated warmup window (3 minutes for standard matchmaking, 10 minutes for structured tournaments).
  • AFK / Declined: Triggered if you fail to click the "Accept" button when a match queue pops.

What is the Elo penalty for a Leaver?

When flagged as a "Leaver" during a match, your Elo calculations are heavily penalized:

  • If your team loses: You receive **double the standard Elo deduction** (~50 Elo loss).
  • If your team wins: You receive only **50% of the calculated Elo gain** (~12 Elo gain).
